the big island

Admire erupting volcanoes, sample local food, or take a late-night dive to see manta rays all on the Big Island. Whether you prefer a large hotel suite in Kona or a small house in Hilo, we can arrange it for you.

Dive with Manta Rays

Let a guide take you and your family or friends on a 2 and a half hour night dive to swim with manta rays. These gentle giants are sure to impress everyone. All equipment and gear is provided, and we will normally depart an hour after sunset.

Fly over Kilauea Volcano

On a 90 minute flight, an experienced guide will fly you over fresh lava flows and erupting mounds of molten rock. Watch it flow to the sea where it boils and hisses with the cold ocean water. Panoramic views of the ocean and the island will surround you.

Go on a Local Food Tour

A half day excursion, you will have the chance to sample sweet pastries, mahi mahi, poi (sweet taro paste), guavas, shave ice, and of course, many different kinds of poke (raw fish). Finish the day with a seafood buffet overlooking a stunning sunset.
