oahu

Here is an urban metropolis in the midst of paradise. Stay in downtown Honolulu, or let us help you find housing elsewhere on the island. Some of the best beaches in Hawaii are located here, so make sure to check them out!

Coffee and Macadamia Nut Tour

Visit a family-owned farm hidden away in the green valleys to sample all different types of coffee and macadamia nuts. A 45 minute tour will take you through the creation process from start to finish. Complementary gifts will be offered at the end.

Hike the Haiku Stairs

This hike is not for the faint-hearted, as the steps are narrow and there are few safety measures in place. However, the summit will reward visitors with a panoramic view of the entire island. Make sure to dress warmly as it can be windy at the top.

Swim with Sea Turtles

Turtle Town, aptly named, is on the West side of the island. While many resorts abound, a guide will take you on a 3 hour trip to lesser known places so that you can admire the gentle giants in seclusion. Turtles are federally protected by state law, so please do not reach out and try to touch them.
